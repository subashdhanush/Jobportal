About Us

Myjob, An innovation to simplify Job and Candidate search for desirables. We have designed the world's most effortless job search service.

We focused on the non-executive category and created the best match for job seekers and trustworthy employers. Our services are classified into Fulltime Jobs, Part-time Jobs, Contract Jobs, Temporary Jobs, Permanent Jobs, Remote Jobs, and Third-party Jobs for job seekers. We offer resume writing, interview tips, and career tips for achieving a dream Job.

You can find a job next to home to anywhere in Singapore. We aim to satisfy Job seekers' desire about location, salary, industry, role, flexible hours, remote working.

Mission

Our mission has to organize the Job search information and make it simply accessible. The Job Seekers to be hired and The Employers can hire desired talent easily.  

As technology continues to evolve, our commitment will always be the same. Helping all Job seekers and Employers to find relevant information about Singapore Jobs.

Vision

Our vision is to integrate search technology in Job and candidate search. We minimize the complexity of the hiring process and reduce the efforts of Job seekers and Companies. Our Technology-driven processes can possible things simple to simpler and forward to simplest.   

Why We are the best

Uniquely qualified

Get your job in front of Recruter members who are active on our network, engaged in their careers, and open to new opportunities.

Targeted matches

Our network gives us a deep, up-to-date, and insightful dataset of professionals. We use that data to match your role to the most qualified professionals.

Only pay for results

Pay only when candidates view your job post. Tell us your budget, and we can estimate how many applicants you'll receive.
